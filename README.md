# About

The Financial Performance Dashboard provides a clear view of a company‚Äôs overall financial health. It tracks key metrics such as total sales, total profit, total units sold, and profit margin. The dashboard includes visual charts showing sales trends by year, monthly profit performance, and profit distribution across different countries. It also highlights which products and discount bands contribute most to sales and profits. With this dashboard, users can easily identify top-performing regions and products, monitor changes in financial results over time, and make informed business decisions. The filters for country, year, segment, and product allow users to analyze data from different perspectives and gain deeper insights into company performance.

## Overview:

The Financial Performance Dashboard offers a comprehensive and interactive summary of a company‚Äôs financial outcomes. It presents key indicators such as total sales of 118.7M, total profit of 18.45M, total units sold of 1.13M, and an overall profit margin of 15.54%. These figures help evaluate the organization‚Äôs profitability and sales efficiency.
The dashboard features multiple visualizations that make data easy to understand at a glance. A line chart shows total sales growth by year, while a monthly profit trend chart highlights seasonal patterns or fluctuations. The bar chart on profit by country reveals top-performing regions, with France, Germany, and Canada leading. Pie charts display sales distribution by product and discount band, helping identify which products or discount levels drive more revenue.
With interactive filters for country, year, segment, and product, users can customize their view and analyze specific areas of interest. This dashboard helps managers, analysts, and decision-makers monitor business performance, track progress toward goals, and make strategic data-driven decisions efficiently.
